Massage Types

Russian Massage

Russian massage is our signature massage and it is a combination of different styles we have developed over the years and is extremely unique. It is hard to describe so you would just have to get one and be the judge!!

Swedish Massage

Swedish massage is a very common form of massage that focuses on relaxing the body and usually involves smooth long fluid strokes.

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massage therapy is similar to Swedish massage, but the deeper pressure is beneficial in releasing chronic muscle tension.

Lomi-Lomi

This is a unique Hawaiian technique and only a few truly know how to perform it. It is characterized by using long continuous strokes, combined with loving touch, and extremely relaxing.

Light Touch Massage

This form of massage is similar to Swedish but it feels extremely light to the body.
